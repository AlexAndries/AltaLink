DEFINE

What is AIM?

AIM is a made-for-AltaLink process improvement approach. It is a simple, 4-stage process that takes into consideration our business and our culture and uses best practices and core concepts from other well-known improvement methodologies like Lean and Six Sigma. AIM will allow everyone at AltaLink to improve processes. Go to the Toolkit to get started.

WHAT IS A PROCESS?

A process is a series of actions that produce something or lead to a particular result. Any work can be considered a process, for example: scheduling maintenance, onboarding new employees, or even setting up a meeting.

WHY ARE WE IMPROVING PROCESSES?

In most areas, AltaLink is not a process-oriented organization . This means many of us do similar tasks in different ways. It also means we frequently rely on the knowledge of others to get our jobs done. These ways of doing things can lead to duplication, inefficiency, inconsistency and “silo” thinking.

WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS OF IMPROVED PROCESSES?

Some of the benefits of becoming process-oriented include:

  • Greater role clarity
  • More efficient work
  • Reduced number of silos
  • Reduced duplication
  • More consistency in deliverables
  • Enhanced quality
  • Improved business sustainability
  • Increased customer satisfaction

BECOMING MORE PROCESS-ORIENTED ALSO REDUCES WASTE. WHAT DO WE MEAN BY WASTE?

  • Over-processing (too many sign-offs, exceeding requests)
  • Transportation (forms moving between departments)
  • Motion (personal movement)
  • Inventory (overfull inboxes)
  • Waiting (delays between steps)
  • Defects (insufficient or wrong information, necessitating additional work)
  • Overproduction (producing more than is needed)
  • Underutilized human capability (underused skills)
  • Reprioritization (changing priorities mid-stream)

WHAT IS EXPECTED OF ME?

Please use AIM when improving processes in your role or with your team. Note: AIM shares key concepts with the Diamond methodology, which has been at AltaLink for a number of years. If you have documented or improved processes using that the Diamond methodology they are still valid, but going forward, all AltaLink employees should use AIM.

WHAT IS THE GOAL OF AIM?

The goal of AIM is to improve business results by having employees and teams learn, apply and exercise a consistent approach for process improvement.

WHERE CAN I GET HELP WITH AIM?

While you are expected to drive process improvement work based on your functional business plans and PRD goals, the AIM team is here to help with training and support.
